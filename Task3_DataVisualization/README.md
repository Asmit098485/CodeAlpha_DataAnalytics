# 📈 Key Insights

- Most passengers did not survive.
- Third-class passengers were the largest group.
- Male passengers were more than female passengers.
- Female passengers had a higher survival rate.
- First-class passengers survived more often than third-class passengers.
- Most passengers were between 20 and 40 years old.
- Ticket fares varied significantly.
- Southampton (S) was the busiest embarkation port.
- The Fare column contains several outliers.
- Correlation analysis shows relationships among numerical features.
